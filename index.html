Knowing EcoYuva Foundation

The EcoYuva Foundation, founded in 2025 by a dedicated group of young social workers, is devoted to uplifting underprivileged children, women, and rural communities. Our comprehensive strategy includes environment, education, skill-building, health programs, sustainable practices, sports inclusion, fostering social and market awareness, and agritourism.

We actively implement our mission through various initiatives that enhance human well-being. Currently, we operate four key drives — Sports, Awareness, Education, and Skill drives — in our operational area of Jaipur, Rajasthan. We believe that living in a better environment with access to basic amenities should be a fundamental right, not a privilege. Our awareness drive plays a crucial role in sensitizing people to the challenges faced by marginalized communities and the steps needed to improve their circumstances.

Our focus is on fostering self-reliance, particularly by giving a platform to underprivileged children through sports and education, empowering women through skill development, menstrual hygiene education, local resource development, and improved access to higher education and technical training. While our primary efforts are concentrated in villages around Jaipur, Rajasthan, we aspire to expand our impact continually.

Our Mission

The EcoYuva Foundation is committed to uplifting underprivileged children, youth, women, and rural communities through a holistic approach that includes:

  • Tailored Education: Delivering education that meets the specific needs of the community.
  • Health and Nutrition Awareness: Raising awareness about vital health and nutrition practices.
  • Sustainable Practices: Encouraging environmentally friendly and sustainable living.
  • Practical Skill Development: Providing market-oriented training to support livelihoods.
  • Resource Accessibility: Supplying essential resources to promote self-reliance.
  • Community Strengthening: Building resilient communities for a sustainable future.
  • Agritourism: Providing farmers with skills to turn their farms into tourism spots and increase income through agriculture.

In addition, we aim to:

  • Employ innovative methodologies and technology to maximize social impact.
  • Maintain high standards of governance.
  • Connect corporate goals with social development efforts.
  • Promote civic engagement by sensitizing youth and citizens to drive change.

The EcoYuva Foundation seeks to inspire a transformative shift in humanity. Our mission is to equip individuals, communities, and nations with the knowledge, skills, and resources necessary for a sustainable and just world. We are dedicated to:

  • Exemplary Governance: Adhering to the highest governance standards.
  • Innovative Solutions: Using advanced knowledge and technology to drive progress.
  • Scalable Impact: Establishing ourselves as a leading organization with widespread, scalable influence.
